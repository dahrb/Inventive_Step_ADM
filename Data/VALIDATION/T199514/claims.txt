1. À system (20) for supplying power to an LED load (10), the system (20)
comprising:

a LED driver module (30) operable to regulate a flow of a LED current (Пр)
through the LED load (10) as a function of a temperature and current-dependent feedback
signal (TDFS): and

a current controller module (60) in electric communication with said LED
driver module (30) to communicate the temperature and current-dependent feedback signal
(TDFS) to said LED driver module (10),
wherein said current controller module (60) is operable to generate the temperature and
current-dependent feedback signal (TDFB) as a function of an operating temperature of the
LED load (10) and the flow of the LED current (Irp) through the LED load (10), and
wherein said current controller module (600) includes:

means for generating a temperature feedback signal (Vrr) as a function of a
sensed operating temperature of the LED load (10); wherein the temperature feedback signal
is a temperature feedback voltage (Vrr) and said current controller module (600) includes:

a first operational amplifier (U1) operable to generate the temperature
feedback voltage (Vrr) as a function of the operating temperature of the LED load (10);

means for generating a current feedback signal (Vcr) as a function of a sensed
flow of the LED current (Irsp) through the LED load (10); wherein the current feedback
signal is a current feedback voltage (Vcr) and said current controller module (60) includes:

a second operational amplifier (U2) operable to generate the current feedback
voltage (Vcr) as a function of the flow of the LED current (Пер) through the LED load (10);
and

means for mixing the temperature feedback signal (Vrr) and the current
feedback signal (Vcr) to yield the temperature and current-dependent feedback signal
(TDFB), wherein the temperature and current-dependent feedback signal (TDFB) is а
feedback voltage generated as a mixture of the temperature feedback voltage (Vrr) and the

current feedback voltage (Vcr).

15. A method for supplying power to an LED load (10), the method comprising:

generating a current-sensing signal (CSS) indicative of a flow of a LED
current (Irsp) through the LED load (10);

generating a current feedback voltage (Vcr) based on said current-sensing
signal (CSS);

generating a temperature-sensing signal (TSS) indicative of an operational

temperature of the LED load (10);

generating a temperature feedback voltage (Vrr) based on said temperature-
sensing signal (TSS);
mixing the temperature feedback voltage (Vrr) and the current feedback
voltage (Vcr) to yield a temperature and current-dependent feedback voltage (TDFB); and
regulating the flow of the LED current (Пр) through the LED load (10) as a

function of said temperature and current-dependent feedback voltage (TDFB).
