CLAIMS
1. A tubular delivery system comprising:

a covering comprising a first compartment, and comprising a poly(glycolic acid) (PGA)
mesh , the covering including a physical attachment mechanism selected from a wrap, a suture, a
wire, a string, an elastic band, a cable, or a cable tie;

a first substance comprising a mixture of surface demineralized bone chips and
demineralized bone fibers provided within the covering;

wherein the covering retains the substance for placement at a surgical site and facilitates
transfer of the substance or of surrounding materials, actively or passively, upon implantation.

2. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the covering retains particles of the substance in
spatial proximity to one another.

3. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the covering comprises an actively releasing material
and is configured to release such material during degradation of the covering.

4. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the tubular covering is configured for subdivision into
smaller tubular compartments in an operating room.

5. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the covering comprises an elongated containment
portion and first and second ends and wherein the attachment mechanism is provided at
at least one of the first and second ends.

6. The delivery system of claim 5, wherein the covering further comprises a seal at at least one of
the first and second ends.

7. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the attachment mechanism is integral to the
covering.

8. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the attachment mechanism is provided
separately from the covering and is attached to the covering for use.

9. The delivery system of claim 1, further comprising a tension band.

10. The delivery system of claim 1, wherein the attachment mechanism cooperates
with an attachment mechanism of a second covering.
