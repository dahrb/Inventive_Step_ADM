CLAIMS for the main request

1. A method of manufacturing an external force detec-

tion sensor, the method comprising:

forming a recessed part (16; 28) on a back surface
side (3b) of an element substrate (3) to form a membrane

(17) on a front side (3a),

providing an etching stop layer (18) comprising an
electrically conductive material. on a top surface (16a) of

the recessed part (16) of said element substrate (3),

joining the back surface side of said element sub-

strate (3) with a support substrate (2), and

forming a sensor element by dry etching of the mem-

brane (17) of said element substrate (3),

wherein the element substrate (3) is anodically joined

with the support substrate (2).

2. The method according to claim 1, wherein the re-
cessed part (16) is formed in a center part of a back sur-

face of the element substrate (3).

3. The method according to claim 1, further comprising
forming a recessed part (27) on the front side (3a) of an

element substrate (3).

4, The method according to one of claims 1 to 3 ,
wherein the element substrate (3) is formed of a silicon
material, and the support substrate (2) is formed of a

glass material.

5. The method according to one of claims 1 to 4,
